WHAT IS LONGHORN?

After the resounding success of LongHorn BBQ 2022, we are so far ahead in planning LongHorn BBQ 2023 that we can open registration for this year's Barbeque competition, with participants & judges from all over Europe.

As a new addition in 2023, LongHorn BBQ will also be the Danish Championship in American Barbeque, so there are more titles to go home with.

DM in American Barbeque is not an open championship, but it is the best placed Danish Team that will be able to boast with the title and not least with an invitation to Jack Daniel's World Championship Invitational BBQ, which is the World Championship in real American BBQ. The Teams that participate here, are only the best from the BBQ arena in the whole world, so it is not only a huge experience but also a huge opportunity to really put your Team on the BBQ map, not only as Danish Champion in American Barbeque, but also as a participant in Jack Daniel's World Championship Invitational BBQ, probably one of the most amazing experiences you can get as a Team. Stay tuned to this page, and follow along as more and more information about the weekend becomes available.
